How to Download Your Result

Follow these simple steps to view and download your marksheet:

  1. Enter your School Code, Class, and Roll Number in the form below.
  2. Click the "View Result" button to generate your marksheet.
  3. Once the marksheet is displayed, click the "Download Marksheet (PDF)" button.
  4. The system will first convert the marksheet into a high-quality image and then save it as a PDF file for you to download.
